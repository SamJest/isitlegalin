Legal position

Current starter summary

Portugal treats cannabis and other controlled substances as a regulated import matter. INFARMED provides a specific cannabis import certificate and the Portuguese travellers guide says medicines must be limited to personal needs and accompanied by the relevant prescription.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Carry only the quantity needed for personal use, have the prescription, and obtain any INFARMED import documentation required for the cannabis-based medicine.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

The official Portuguese framework treats medicinal cannabis as a controlled and documented import, not as a general free traveller good.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

No penalty summary has been added yet.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

INFARMED regulates controlled substances and the relevant cannabis-import documentation, while customs applies traveller-import rules.
