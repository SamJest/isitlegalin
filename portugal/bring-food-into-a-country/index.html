Legal position

Current starter summary

Portugal treats many food categories as restricted traveller goods. The Portuguese customs guidance says prohibited or restricted goods can include foodstuffs, products of animal origin, plant products, fruits and seeds.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Check the category of food and comply with the relevant customs and sanitary rules before travel.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

The answer depends on the type of food and where it is coming from.
