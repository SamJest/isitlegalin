Legal position

Current starter summary

Norway allows tourists to bring ordinary personal belongings temporarily, but goods above the value limit or goods that are not treated as personal travel effects must be declared.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Declare the goods if they exceed the value limit or are not covered as ordinary tourist belongings.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

Computers, phones and similar personal belongings carried by tourists can be treated differently from imported goods for permanent use.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

No penalty summary has been added yet.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

Norwegian Customs ties declaration duties to the value limit and whether the item counts as ordinary tourist property.
