Legal position

Current starter summary

Downloading pirated movies through infringing file sharing can breach copyright in New Zealand.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Use a licensed service or get the rights holder's permission before downloading the film.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

Licensed downloads and other authorised copies are outside this row.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

Rights owners can issue file-sharing notices and may seek orders through the Copyright Tribunal under the special file-sharing regime.
