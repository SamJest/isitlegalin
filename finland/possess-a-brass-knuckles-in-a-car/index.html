Legal position

Current starter summary

Finland’s Public Order Act prohibits the manufacture, importation, trading, and possession in a public place or a vehicle in a public place of brass knuckles.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Do not buy, carry, or keep brass knuckles in a vehicle in a public place.

Exceptions

The Act contains narrow collector-related rules for historic or artistic items.
