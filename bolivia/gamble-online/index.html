Legal position

Current starter summary

Bolivia's gambling regulator says profit-making betting is prohibited nationally and warns that there are no legal online sports betting services in Bolivia. Online gambling should not be treated as lawful from the official source reviewed.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Do not rely on betting websites or apps promoted online as being legal in Bolivia.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

No official exception for legal online sports betting was identified in the source reviewed.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

The AJ says profit-making betting is prohibited nationally, but the reviewed communication does not set one fixed penalty for a generic consumer online-gambling row.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

The AJ publicly warns that online sports betting sites and apps are not legal in Bolivia and tells users to report unlawful betting activity.

bring electronics without declaring them

Bolivia's traveler regime lets passengers bring personal effects and listed electronic items as accompanied baggage, and new personal-use goods can be duty-free up to the traveler allowance. Electronics that fall outside the allowance or are subject to duties must be declared.
