Legal position

Current starter summary

Turkey allows only limited tobacco quantities within passenger exemptions. Tobacco above those limits is not exempt and should be declared and taxed.

Conditions

What would need to be true

The passenger tobacco allowance is 600 cigarettes, 100 cigarillos, 50 cigars, 250 grams of cut tobacco and 250 grams of pipe tobacco, and under-18 travellers cannot use the tobacco exemption.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

Tobacco within the stated passenger allowance is exempt.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

No penalty summary has been entered yet.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

The Ministry of Trade customs administration checks passenger tobacco allowances and declarations.

Turkey allows passenger gift goods up to EUR 430 per traveller, or EUR 150 for travellers under 15, and taxes goods above that amount if they stay within the traveller limit. Goods that must be declared should go through the red lane.
