Legal position

Current starter summary

Switzerland allows entry with certain medicinal products, including controlled medicines, but Swissmedic applies quantity and document rules. The Swissmedic traveller guidance says medical cannabis can be brought in subject to the official narcotics-traveller rules.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Keep the medicine for personal treatment, stay within the official quantity limit and carry the required certificate or medical proof.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

Controlled medicines are treated more strictly than ordinary medicines.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

No penalty summary has been entered yet.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

Swissmedic treats traveller narcotic medicines as lawful only within the stated quantity and documentation rules.
