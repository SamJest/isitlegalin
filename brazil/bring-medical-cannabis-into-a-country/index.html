Legal position

Current starter summary

Brazil does not allow ordinary traveler carriage of medical cannabis as a normal baggage item. Anvisa says importing cannabis-derived products for personal treatment requires prior authorization and a prescription.

Conditions

What would need to be true

You need Anvisa authorization and a prescription before importation for personal treatment.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

The source checked here only supports exceptional authorized importation for personal use.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

No penalty summary has been added yet.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

Anvisa and border health and customs controls supervise the importation of controlled cannabis-derived products.

bring food into a country
