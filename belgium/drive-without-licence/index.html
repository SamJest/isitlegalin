Legal position

Current starter summary

Belgian road rules require a valid driving licence to drive. Belgian Justice has said road traffic offenders, including people driving without a valid licence, face tougher sanctions and imprisonment can apply.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Hold a valid licence recognized for the vehicle and situation before driving in Belgium.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

The official sources reviewed here do not provide a general exception allowing ordinary driving without a valid licence.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

The exact sanction depends on the circumstances, but Belgian Justice says imprisonment can also apply for driving without a valid licence.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

Police and criminal courts enforce driving-licence offences under Belgian road traffic law.
