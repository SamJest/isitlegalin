Legal position

Current starter summary

Online gambling is allowed in Belgium only through operators licensed by the Belgian Gaming Commission. Official Commission pages publish licence data and blocked illegal gambling sites.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Use a site that appears in the Belgian Gaming Commission's current licence data and complies with Belgian gambling rules.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

Using an illegal or blocked site falls outside the licensed regime.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

The official Gaming Commission stop page says illegal gambling can be punishable, but the exact penalty depends on the conduct and offence.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

The Gaming Commission publishes licence lists and blocks illegal gambling sites in Belgium.
