Legal position

Current starter summary

Belgium's data protection authority says it is in principle prohibited to record electronic conversations, including professional phone conversations, unless a recognized exception applies.

Conditions

What would need to be true

All persons involved normally need to consent unless a specific technical or legal exception applies, such as certain call-center quality control or lawful recording of commercial transactions.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

The Belgian DPA identifies exceptions for call-center quality monitoring, lawful recording of commercial transactions, and situations specifically authorized by law.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

No penalty summary has been added yet.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

Employers must also comply with GDPR duties, including transparency, purpose limitation and storage information for the parties involved.
