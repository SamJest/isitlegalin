Legal position

Current starter summary

Belgium allows travellers to bring goods within customs traveller allowances, but duty-free treatment is limited by route, value and product category. Goods above the allowance are not automatically exempt.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Keep within the applicable value and quantity thresholds and follow customs declaration rules when required.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

Different limits apply depending on whether goods are arriving from within or outside the EU and depending on the transport mode.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

No penalty summary has been added yet.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

Belgian customs applies the traveller allowance rules and determines when declaration and duties are required.
