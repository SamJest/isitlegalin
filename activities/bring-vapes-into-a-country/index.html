Starter comparison cards

Argentina

ANMAT says electronic cigarettes are prohibited for importation, distribution and commercialization in Argentina. Bringing vapes into Argentina should therefore not be treated as permitted ordinary import.

Noverified

Brazil

Brazil bans electronic smoking devices. Anvisa says it is prohibited to manufacture, sell, import, advertise or distribute all types of e-cigarettes, vapes, pods and similar devices.

Noverified

Egypt

Egypt’s official medicine-travel guidance lists electronic cigarettes among the prohibited products for travellers.

Noverified

Mexico

Mexico’s customs authority says electronic cigarettes and personal vaporization devices, including heated-tobacco and similar nicotine or non-nicotine systems, are goods you cannot bring into Mexico.

Noverified

Suriname

Suriname's public health ministry says importing e-cigarettes and other tobacco products is prohibited under article 13 of the Tobacco Act. Bringing vapes into Suriname should not be treated as lawful.

Noverified

Taiwan

Taiwan Customs states that passengers are not allowed to carry e-cigarettes and e-liquids into Taiwan. Even transit passengers with short layovers must declare them for deposit rather than enter with them freely.

Noverified

Thailand

Thai Customs states that electronic cigarettes and baraku are prohibited goods for importation into Thailand. Bringing vapes into Thailand should not be treated as permitted ordinary import.

Noverified

Vietnam

Vietnam has banned business activities related to e-cigarettes and heated-tobacco products, and official guidance describes a prohibition on production, trade, importation, harbouring, transportation and use. Bringing vapes into Vietnam should not be treated as lawful traveller entry.

Noverified
