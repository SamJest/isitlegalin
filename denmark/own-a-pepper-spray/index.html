Legal position

Current starter summary

In Denmark you need police permission to buy and have pepper spray. Danish police say pepper-spray permits are generally only given to adults with a special need for protection.

Conditions

What would need to be true

A permit is required; applicants must be at least 18 and are assessed for a concrete risk of violent attack and suitability to use pepper spray.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

Police say permits are generally limited to people with a special protection need, such as stalking or certain conflict situations.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

Unauthorised acquisition or possession can be punished under Denmark's weapons rules; the official sources reviewed do not state one fixed penalty for every scenario.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

The police administer the permit system and enforce the weapons rules.
