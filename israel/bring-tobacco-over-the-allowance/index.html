Legal position

Current starter summary

Israel’s customs guide allows travellers aged 18 and over up to 200 cigarettes or 250 grams of other tobacco products. Tobacco above that is outside the exemption.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Stay within the allowance and use the correct customs channel if you exceed it.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

The allowance applies only to travellers aged 18 and over.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

Goods above the exempt quantity or amount are not tax-exempt and must go through customs assessment in Israel.
