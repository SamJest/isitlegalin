Frequently Asked Questions

What does "legal", "restricted", or "illegal" mean?

Legal means the activity is generally permitted under national law. Restricted means the activity may require permits, licensing, or have limitations. Illegal means the activity is prohibited or treated as a criminal offence.

Where does the legal information come from?

Information is compiled from legislation, legal codes, regulatory agencies, and publicly available government guidance.
