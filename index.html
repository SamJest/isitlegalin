Understanding Laws Across Countries

Laws differ dramatically between countries. An activity that is completely legal in one jurisdiction may be restricted or prohibited in another depending on national legislation and regulatory policy.

IsItLegalIn provides a structured global legality database designed to help people quickly understand how laws differ between jurisdictions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does "legal", "restricted", or "illegal" mean?

Legal means the activity is generally permitted under national law. Restricted means permits or limitations may apply. Illegal means the activity is prohibited.

Where does the legal information come from?

Information is compiled from legislation, regulatory guidance, and publicly available government sources.
