Legal position

Current starter summary

Online gambling in France is lawful only through operators authorised in France and only for categories the regime permits.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Check both the operator and the product because France authorises only parts of the online market.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

Online casino style offers and other unauthorised products do not become lawful just because the site is easy to access from France.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

The ANJ consumer guidance focuses on illegal site risks and loss of legal protections rather than a simple fixed player penalty.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

The ANJ publishes authorised operators and treats unauthorised offers as illegal offers outside the French framework.
