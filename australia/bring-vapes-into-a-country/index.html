Legal position

Current starter summary

Australia allows only a narrow travellers exemption for small quantities of therapeutic vapes and otherwise treats vaping goods as prohibited imports without prior permission.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Travellers by ship or aircraft can bring up to 2 vapes 20 accessories and 200 mL of vape substance only for the treatment of the traveller or someone they are caring for on the same journey.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

Vapes containing controlled drugs such as cannabis are outside the travellers exemption and state or territory possession rules can still be stricter.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

ABF says unlawful prohibited imports may be seized and can attract serious penalties and the TGA says unlawful personal imports may be seized and destroyed.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

ABF screens imports at the border and the TGA framework controls lawful supply.
