Legal position

Current starter summary

Zimbabwe has a licensed gambling framework under the Lotteries and Gaming Board, so online gambling depends on whether the activity and operator are lawfully licensed within that framework.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Use only operators that are licensed by the relevant Zimbabwe gambling authorities.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

The official source reviewed here describes the licensing framework but does not give a flat consumer yes or no for every online gambling product.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

Unlicensed gambling activity can face regulatory enforcement under Zimbabwe’s gambling regime, but the exact sanction depends on the specific law and licence issue.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

The Lotteries and Gaming Board describes itself as the body licensing and regulating gambling activities in Zimbabwe.
