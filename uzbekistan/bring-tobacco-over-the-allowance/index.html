Legal position

Current starter summary

Uzbekistan allows only limited duty-free tobacco in passenger baggage. Tobacco above the allowance is outside the relief and should not be treated as freely importable without customs formalities.

Conditions

What would need to be true

The duty-free limit is up to 10 packages of tobacco products within the passenger customs rules.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

Tobacco within the official duty-free allowance can enter under the passenger rules.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

Tobacco outside the allowance is subject to ordinary customs payments and declaration requirements rather than the duty-free relief.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

Customs officers apply the passenger allowance rules and duty-free limits at the border.
