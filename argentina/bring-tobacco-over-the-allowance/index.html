Legal position

Current starter summary

Argentina's baggage rules set duty-free limits for tobacco products such as cigarettes and cigars depending on the travel route. Bringing more than the allowance takes you outside the automatic exemption.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Declare excess tobacco and comply with the applicable customs duties and baggage rules.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

Tobacco within the applicable baggage allowance can enter under the relevant exemption.

bring electronics without declaring them
