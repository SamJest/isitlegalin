Legal position

Current starter summary

Argentina allows patients to request ANMAT authorization to import a cannabinoid or cannabis-derived product needed for treatment when it is not marketed in Argentina. Official guidance says the import route is tied to medical indication and the exception-access regime.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Carry the required ANMAT authorization and supporting medical documentation before bringing the product into Argentina.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

This is an exception-access treatment route, not a general unrestricted right to carry cannabis products.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

No penalty summary has been added yet.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

Entry is handled through the ANMAT authorization process for cannabinoid and cannabis-derived medicines.
