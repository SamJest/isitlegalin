What rules or paperwork do I need to record phone calls in Germany?

No. Recording phone calls is generally illegal in Germany, unless a narrow exception or legal justification clearly applies.

What conditions matter for recording phone calls in Germany?

All participants must agree to recording. Secret recording is prohibited. Private communications are protected.

Criminal fines. Possible imprisonment. Germany applies strict consent rules to communications.
