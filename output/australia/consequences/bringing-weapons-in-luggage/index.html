What happens if I bring weapons in luggage in Australia?

Structured guidance for Australia | Updated 1 January 2026

Decision summary

No. Bringing weapons in luggage is generally illegal in Australia, unless a narrow exception or legal justification clearly applies.

Start from prohibition and only move off that position if a clear exception fits your facts.

Most weapons are prohibited from being brought into Australia, with very strict import controls. Use the penalties and enforcement notes below to judge the practical downside of getting this wrong.
