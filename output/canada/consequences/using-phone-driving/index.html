FAQ

Common questions

What happens if I use a phone while driving in Canada?

No. Using a phone while driving is generally illegal in Canada, unless a narrow exception or legal justification clearly applies.

What conditions matter for using a phone while driving in Canada?

Handheld use is prohibited. Applies to calls, texting, and apps. Drivers must pull over safely to use.

Are there exceptions for using a phone while driving in Canada?

Hands-free systems are allowed. Emergency calls may be permitted.

What are the penalties for using a phone while driving in Canada?

Varies by province. Rare. Demerit points usually apply.

How is using a phone while driving enforced in Canada?

Police actively enforce distracted driving laws with fines and points.
