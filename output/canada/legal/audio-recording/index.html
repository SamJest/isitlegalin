Is it legal to record conversations in Canada?

Structured guidance for Canada | Updated 1 January 2026

Legal Guarded Source trail 2

Decision summary

Yes. Recording conversations is generally legal in Canada, although specific conditions or restrictions may still apply.

The baseline answer is permissive, but the listed conditions and practical limits still matter.

Canada generally follows a one-party consent rule, meaning you can record a conversation if you are part of it. Recording others without involvement is more restricted. Review the conditions, exceptions, penalties, and enforcement notes below before relying on the headline answer.
