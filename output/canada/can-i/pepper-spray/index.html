Decision summary

Depends. In Canada, carrying pepper spray may be legal depending on the circumstances, purpose, or local restrictions.

Small factual differences can change the result, so treat the conditions and exceptions as part of the answer.

Pepper spray for use against humans is generally prohibited in Canada, but animal deterrent sprays may be legal if not carried for self-defence against people. Use the conditions and exceptions below to check whether your exact situation still fits the rule.
