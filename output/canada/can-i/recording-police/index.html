Can I film police officers in Canada?

Yes. Filming police officers is generally legal in Canada, although specific conditions or restrictions may still apply.

What conditions matter for filming police officers in Canada?

You must keep a safe distance. You cannot interfere with police operations. Recording should take place in public areas.
