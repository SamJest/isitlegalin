Decision summary

Depends. In the United States, using hidden cameras may be legal depending on the circumstances, purpose, or local restrictions.

Small factual differences can change the result, so treat the conditions and exceptions as part of the answer.

Hidden cameras can be legal in some situations in the United States, but they become risky or illegal when they record people in private spaces. State law and the location of the recording matter a lot. Use the penalties and enforcement notes below to judge the practical downside of getting this wrong.
