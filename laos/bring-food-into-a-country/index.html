Legal position

Current starter summary

Importing food into Laos is regulated. The Lao Trade Portal says food and beverage imports require an import permit from the Ministry of Health and the permit must be presented with the other border documents.

Conditions

What would need to be true

You need the required Ministry of Health import permit and supporting documents for the food import.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

The official source reviewed is an import-procedure page and does not create a general traveller exception for undeclared food.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

Failure to present the required import permit may result in rejection or confiscation of the goods.

Penalty row: barePenalty data updated: 2026-04-13

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

Border officials can require the import permit and other documents before allowing the food import.
