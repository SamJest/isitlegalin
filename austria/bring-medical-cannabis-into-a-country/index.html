Legal position

Current starter summary

Austria allows travellers to enter with certain medicines for personal use, including controlled medicines, but the official health guidance imposes document and quantity limits.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Carry proof of personal medical need and follow the official certificate or prescription rules for controlled medicines.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

For addictive or narcotic medicines, the official guidance applies different rules depending on whether the supply is up to five days or up to thirty days.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

No penalty summary has been added yet.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

Austria’s health ministry guidance treats traveller medicines as lawful only within the stated documentation and quantity limits.
