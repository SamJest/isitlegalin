Legal position

Current starter summary

Austria allows travellers to bring personal goods, but goods that exceed allowances or do not qualify as ordinary travel effects must be declared on entry from outside the EU.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Declare the goods if they exceed the traveller allowances or are not covered as normal personal travel effects.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

Ordinary personal belongings that clearly fall within traveller rules are treated differently from new or higher-value goods.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

No penalty summary has been added yet.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

Austria’s customs guidance links declaration duties to traveller allowances and the character of the goods.
