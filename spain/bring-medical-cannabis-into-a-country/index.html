Legal position

Current starter summary

Medical cannabis can only be brought into Spain if it fits the official medicines rules for travellers and any required permit for narcotic or psychotropic medicines has been obtained.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Travellers entering Spain with narcotic or psychotropic medicines must request the corresponding permit from AEMPS, and the amount is generally limited to treatment for up to three months.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

Ordinary medicines for personal treatment are treated differently from narcotic or psychotropic medicines, which face extra permit requirements.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

Penalty exposure depends on the exact medicines and customs breach if a required traveller permit has not been obtained.

Penalty row: barePenalty data updated: 2026-04-12

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

AEMPS publishes the traveller-medicines rules and permit requirements for narcotic and psychotropic medicines entering Spain.
