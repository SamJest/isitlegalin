Legal position

Current starter summary

Using a VPN is not prohibited by the official Spanish materials checked here and government cyber-security guidance recommends VPNs as a security tool.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Using a VPN still has to comply with any other law that applies to the underlying conduct.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

A VPN does not make otherwise unlawful conduct lawful.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

The official materials checked here do not identify a standalone penalty for ordinary VPN use.

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

INCIBE publishes guidance that treats VPNs as a normal way to make communications and teleworking connections more secure.
