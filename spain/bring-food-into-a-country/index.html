Legal position

Current starter summary

You can bring some food into Spain, but food of animal origin from third countries is heavily restricted and some products are prohibited.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Check the category of food before travel and present restricted products for the required border controls.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

The official customs materials contain category-specific exceptions, but there is no flat yes for all foods.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot
