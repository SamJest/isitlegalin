Starter activities in Colombia

These rows prove the country hub works as a useful browse page before the full dataset is researched.

drive without licence

Colombia requires a driving licence to lawfully drive. Ministry of Transport guidance says the licence is the document that authorizes a person to drive, and official ministry guidance cites the traffic-code sanction for driving without having obtained the corresponding licence.

bringing duty free goods

Colombia allows travelers to bring non-commercial personal goods within DIAN’s traveler allowances. Accompanied baggage within the stated traveler allowance can enter without import tax, subject to the value, quantity and traveler-regime rules.

TravelYes

bring electronics without declaring them

Colombia allows travelers to bring personal goods within DIAN traveler allowances, but electronics that fall into the declarable category cannot simply be brought in undeclared. DIAN says omission of a required traveler declaration can lead to seizure and confiscation.

bring food into a country

Food and other animal or plant products are not treated as automatically admissible traveler items in Colombia. ICA says products of animal or plant origin may require sanitary or phytosanitary certificates and can be retained, re-exported, confiscated or destroyed if they do not comply.

buy a brass knuckles

Colombia classifies brass knuckles (manoplas metálicas) as less-lethal kinetic items under Decreto 1563 de 2022. Their commercialization and importation are regulated, and the decree links civilian possession of less-lethal items to marking/registration procedures even though kinetic items do not require a carry permit.

buy a stun gun

Colombia authorizes electric less-lethal devices only within the framework of Decreto 1563 de 2022. Electric devices are only authorized if they meet the decree’s technical specifications, and the civilian possession and carry framework requires marking and a permit process for less-lethal items.
