bringing duty free goods

Bringing duty-free goods into New Zealand is allowed within the traveller concessions and subject to declaration and duty payment rules for any excess.

use a vpn

Using a VPN is not prohibited by the official New Zealand materials checked here and government cyber-security guidance treats VPN services as normal secure remote-access tools.

Digital LawsYes

bring electronics without declaring them

New Zealand does not require every personal electronic device to be declared, but newly acquired or other dutiable goods may need to be declared and duty may be payable.

bring food into a country

You can bring some food into New Zealand, but it must be declared and many foods are restricted or prohibited for biosecurity reasons.

TravelDepends

bring medical cannabis into a country

You can bring medicinal cannabis into New Zealand only if it meets the official medicines rules for controlled drugs and is declared on arrival.

TravelDepends

bring tobacco over the allowance

You may bring tobacco into New Zealand over the duty-free allowance only if you declare it and pay duty and GST on the excess.
