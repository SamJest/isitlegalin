Starter activities in Switzerland

These rows prove the country hub works as a useful browse page before the full dataset is researched.

bring electronics without declaring them

Switzerland’s customs guidance says all goods carried into Switzerland must be declared on entry without being asked. Bringing dutiable electronics in without declaration therefore breaks the border rules.

TravelNo

drive without licence

Driving in Switzerland requires a valid driving licence. The official Swiss guidance says you need a driving licence to drive and that a valid national licence is required to drive a car in Switzerland.

VehiclesNo

bringing duty free goods

Switzerland permits travellers to bring duty-free goods within the official allowances.

TravelYes

buy a pepper spray

Switzerland does not treat pepper spray the same as the irritant self-defence sprays listed as weapons in the border guidance. Official chemicals rules show pepper spray can be supplied to private persons, although not through self-service and subject to specialist-knowledge rules for suppliers.

Self Defence WeaponsYes

download pirated movies

Switzerland’s copyright exception for private use is unusually broad. The Swiss Federal Institute of Intellectual Property says downloading for private use is allowed by law, even from illegal sources.
