Starter activities in Italy

These rows prove the country hub works as a useful browse page before the full dataset is researched.

drive without licence

Italian road law says motor vehicles cannot be driven without having obtained the required driving licence. The official road-code source also gives an administrative fine for driving without ever having obtained a licence.

VehiclesNo

bringing duty free goods

Bringing duty-free goods into Italy is allowed for travellers when the goods fall within the traveller customs exemptions. The official customs charter says customs duty exemption applies to products and consumer goods carried in personal luggage when arriving from non-EU countries within the published limits.

TravelYes

bring medical cannabis into a country

Travellers entering Italy with medicines containing internationally controlled drugs are allowed to carry small quantities for personal medical use only with the required documentation. Italy’s Health Ministry sets a document-based entry procedure rather than a flat yes or no rule.

TravelDepends

bring tobacco over the allowance

Traveller tobacco imports are duty-free only within the published customs allowance. The official customs charter lists 200 cigarettes, or 100 cigarillos, or 50 cigars, or 250 grams of tobacco as the relevant allowance for eligible arrivals.

TravelDepends

buy a pepper spray

Italy allows only compliant oleoresin capsicum self-defence sprays that meet the technical limits in Ministerial Decree No. 103/2011. Products that do not meet those limits remain regulated under the weapons rules.

gamble online

Online gambling in Italy is not a flat yes or no consumer answer. The official ADM remote-gaming pages show that remote gambling is run through ADM-authorized concessionaires, so lawful play depends on using an authorized operator.

monitoring staff

Monitoring staff in Italy is not a flat yes or no answer. The privacy authority says worker-monitoring guarantees remain applicable, and the labour inspectorate runs the authorization service for surveillance, satellite localization and other systems that can enable remote control of workers.
