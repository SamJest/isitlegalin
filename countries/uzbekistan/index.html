Starter activities in Uzbekistan

These rows prove the country hub works as a useful browse page before the full dataset is researched.

bring electronics without declaring them

Uzbekistan lets travelers bring goods within the passenger allowance without listing them in the customs declaration, and it separately limits certain household and computer devices to set quantities and periods. Electronics outside those allowances or limits should be treated as requiring normal customs formalities.

TravelDepends

bring medical cannabis into a country

Uzbekistan's customs guidance allows personal-use import of narcotic drugs and psychotropic substances only within tightly controlled limits and documentation rules. Bringing medical cannabis into Uzbekistan therefore depends on whether it fits the approved lists, quantities and documents rather than being openly allowed.

TravelDepends

bring tobacco over the allowance

Uzbekistan allows only limited duty-free tobacco in passenger baggage. Tobacco above the allowance is outside the relief and should not be treated as freely importable without customs formalities.

TravelDepends

bringing duty free goods

Uzbekistan gives travelers a duty-free value allowance that changes by border type and also applies separate quantitative limits for certain goods. Goods outside those limits are not simply duty-free.

TravelDepends

gamble online

Uzbekistan's 2024 presidential decree allows online risk-based games and bookmaker activity from 1 January 2025 on the basis of licenses issued under the law. Online gambling is therefore not a flat yes or no; it depends on whether the operator is licensed and lawful in Uzbekistan.
