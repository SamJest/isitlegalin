download pirated movies

Downloading copyrighted films from unauthorised sources is treated as piracy and is not a lawful way to access films in France.

Digital LawsNo

stream pirated content

Streaming films or other cultural content from an unauthorised source is treated by the French anti piracy framework as illicit access rather than a lawful alternative to licensed services.

buy a stun gun

Some contact electric shock weapons in France are category D and may be freely acquired by adults but the exact device classification matters.

Self Defence WeaponsDepends

buy a taser

French official sources say only category D weapons can be bought and held freely. Official category pages list contact electric-impulse weapons such as electric batons and some shockers in category D, while an official order notes that an electric-impulse pistol can fall in category B or category D depending on the device and use mode.

Self Defence WeaponsDepends

buy brass knuckles

French official sources classify post-1900 brass knuckles as category A weapons, and category A acquisition and detention are prohibited except for narrow statutory exceptions.
