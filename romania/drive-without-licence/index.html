Legal position

Current starter summary

Romania's Criminal Code makes driving on public roads without a driving licence a criminal offence. Driving without the required licence is therefore not lawful.

Conditions

What would need to be true

Hold the required Romanian or otherwise valid driving licence before driving.

Exceptions

Known carve-outs or edge cases

No exceptions have been entered yet.

Penalties

Penalty snapshot

Romania's Criminal Code provides imprisonment from 1 to 5 years for driving without a licence, and 6 months to 3 years or a fine for certain related licence offences.

Penalty row: barePenalty data updated: 2026-04-13

Enforcement

How this may be enforced

Police and criminal justice authorities enforce the offence under Article 335.
